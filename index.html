Few Pints With the Lads Tonight!

Welcome to the interactive Guinness pint game! Click on the bouncing pints to increase your count and have some fun. This game is inspired by the classic DVD logo screensaver that many of us remember from the early 2000s, but with a fun Irish twist featuring pints of Guinness.

How to Play

  • Watch as Guinness pints bounce around your screen
  • Click on any pint to increase your pint count
  • Each click creates a new bouncing pint
  • Try to get the highest pint count possible!

Features

  • Interactive Guinness pint animation
  • Sound effects on click
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Realistic bouncing physics
  • Pint counter to track your progress

About the Game

This fun interactive game brings back memories of the classic DVD logo screensaver, but with a humorous twist featuring pints of Guinness. Perfect for a quick break or to share with friends for a laugh. The game is completely free to play and works on any modern web browser.
